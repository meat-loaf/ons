Sprite Memory:
0th bit set: Lava slope objects create lava, not alternate grass slopes
1st bit set: slope tiles dont draw dirt (staircases)
2nd bit set: Right-to-Left level: Goal walk is left, flips midway/goalpoint objects
3rd bit set: not currently used

Super GFX Bypass: Sprite Graphics Files
SP1 - Low byte: N/A (Hardcoded to GFX 00)
SP2 - Low byte: N/A (Hardcoded to GFX 01)
SP3 - Low byte: Current Spriteset
SP4 - Low byte: N/A (Graphics populated by spriteset setting)
